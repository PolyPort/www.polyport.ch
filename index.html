Pioneers in crowd-sourced logistics

70% of all transport capacities remain unused on average. With our streets filling up, traffic jams paralyzing our infrastructure on a daily basis and challenging supply chains and our economy, it is time to act. PolyPort wants to utilize unused, private transport capacities to enable sustainable transports and offer an inexpensive last mile delivery solution.

Within two innovative city logistics projects, we bring the sharing economy to transportation "BringBee" & the "Kartoffeltaxi".

BringBee. Crowdsourcing your shopping run.

Why bother going shopping yourself, if thousands of people are on the move every single day? Simply write your shopping list with BringBee and get your shopping bought and delivered by a BringBee nearby for a small reward.
BringBee verifies all members, handles secure payments and offers support and transport insurance.

Read more & join the community!

Kartoffeltaxi. Sustainable deliveries of regional bio-products.

It's not always easy to buy fresh, local and regional products. With the "Kartoffeltaxi" commuters bring the fresh products directly from the producer to your doorstep.

Learn more & enjoy tasty potatoes!
